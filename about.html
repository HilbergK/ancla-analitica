About Ancla Analítica

Learn about our mission, approach, and the team behind our economic and political analyses.

Our Mission

Ancla Analítica was founded with a clear purpose: to make complex economic and political analyses accessible to the general public. We believe that informed citizens make better decisions, and our mission is to contribute to public understanding of important economic and political developments.

Like an anchor that provides stability in turbulent waters, we aim to be a reliable source of analysis in an increasingly complex global landscape. Our name, "Ancla Analítica" (Analytical Anchor), reflects this commitment to providing grounded, stable insights amidst the often chaotic flow of information.

Our Approach

We combine rigorous research methodology with clear communication. Our analyses are based on comprehensive data collection, careful evaluation of sources, and application of established economic and political science frameworks. However, we distinguish ourselves by translating these complex analyses into formats that are accessible to non-specialists.

Our content follows a "trickle-down" approach:

1. We begin with in-depth research reports that maintain academic rigor

2. These are transformed into more accessible website articles

3. Key insights are further distilled into video content

4. Core messages are shared through social media

Our Values

Accessibility: We believe economic and political knowledge should not be confined to academic or policy circles.

Objectivity: While perfect objectivity is impossible, we strive to present balanced analyses that consider multiple perspectives.

Transparency: We clearly cite our sources and explain our methodologies.

Relevance: We focus on issues that have tangible impacts on people's lives and livelihoods.

Our History

Ancla Analítica was established in 2025 in response to growing public interest in understanding the economic and political forces shaping our world. What began as a small project has grown into a dedicated team of analysts, writers, and media specialists working together to bridge the gap between complex analysis and public understanding.
